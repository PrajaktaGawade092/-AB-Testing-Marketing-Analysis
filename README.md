# A/B Testing — Marketing Campaign Analysis

## Objective
Determine whether advertisements drive higher conversion rates compared to PSAs.

## Key Findings
- Ad group: 2.55% conversion vs PSA: 1.79%
- 43.09% lift in conversion rate
- Statistically significant (p = 1.70e-13)
- Best ad exposure: 101–300 ads
- Best days: Monday & Tuesday
- Best hours: 4 PM – 9 PM

## Tools Used
- Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Statsmodels
